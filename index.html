How to use it ?

It is very easy to use this website just follow this steps :-

  1. Click on the numbers beside the X-axis label and erase all then write your co-ordinates for X-axis

  2. Same as X-axis. click on the numbers beside the Y-axis label and erase all then write your co-ordinates for Y-axis

  3. Click on "Title here" besides the Graph title and write your title. You can also edit axis title in the same way.

  4. Now click the button "Update Graph" to update the above graph with your given data.

What does these buttons do ?

These buttons are provided by the Plotly js and they provide few great features.

Those features are

  • At first there is a camera icon button by clicking it , it will a screenshot of the graph and store it in your device.

  • Then there is a icon of magnifing glass with a square in it. This button will enable the crop zoom for the graph. Now you can zoom in any part of your graph.

  • Next there is pan button with a plus symbol like arrow icon. After zooming in any part of the graph click this button to scroll through graph.
