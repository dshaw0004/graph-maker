How to use it ?

It is very easy to use this website just follow this steps :-

  1. Click on the numbers beside the X-axis label and erase all then write your co-ordinates for X-axis

  2. Same as X-axis. click on the numbers beside the Y-axis label and erase all then write your co-ordinates for Y-axis

  3. Click on "Title here" besides the Graph title and write your title. You can also edit axis title in the same way.

  4. Now click the button "Update Graph" to update the above graph with your given data.

What does these buttons do ?

These buttons are provided by the Plotly js and they provide few great features.

Those features are

  • Download button ( camera icon)

    it will take a screenshot of the graph and store it in your device.

  • Zoom button ( magnifing glass icon)

    This button will enable the crop zoom for the graph. Now you can zoom in any part of your graph.

  • Pan button

    After zooming in any part of the graph click this button to scroll through graph.

  • Zoom in button ( Plus icon)

    This will zoom in your graph.

  • Zoom out button (Minus icon )

    This will zoom out your graph.

  • Autoscale button

    This will rescale the graph to its initial state.

  • Strike lines

    Clicking this will toggle strike lines which will a dotted line to each of the axis when hovering over any point
